1. Aim

To build a complete Machine Learning pipeline using a 10-step process to create an AI capable of accurately identifying flower species based on physical measurements.

2. Steps

1. Setup: Workspace configuration.
2. Loading: CSV data import.
3. Cleaning: Noise removal.
4. EDA: Statistical mapping.
5. Features: Defining $X$ and $y$.
6. Split: 80/20 train-test split.
7. Training: Logistic Regression.
8. Visualization: Boundaries.
9. Testing: Accuracy validation.
10. Deployment: .pkl Export.
